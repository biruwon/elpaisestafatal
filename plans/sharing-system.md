# Sharing System Specification v1.0

## Status

Approved

---

# Goal

The sharing system is one of the main growth mechanisms of El País Está Fatal.

Its purpose is not simply to increase traffic.

Its purpose is to make accurate, nuanced and evidence-based arguments as easy to share as simplified or misleading claims.

The website should encourage users to share ideas, evidence and explanations rather than entire articles.

---

# Core Principle

People rarely share long articles.

Instead, they share:

* Charts.
* Statistics.
* Quotes.
* Images.
* Short explanations.
* Arguments they can immediately use in conversations.

Every important content block should therefore become an independent shareable asset.

The article is the source.

The shareable asset is the product.

---

# Design Principles

* Mobile-first.
* One-click sharing.
* Visually attractive.
* Easy to understand in seconds.
* Self-contained.
* Optimised for messaging apps and social media.
* Designed to encourage curiosity rather than confrontation.

---

# What Can Be Shared

Every major content block should be individually shareable.

Examples:

* Main graph.
* Individual chart.
* Statistic card.
* Timeline.
* Map.
* Comparison table.
* Myth or misconception.
* Claim summary.
* Short explanation.

The complete page should also be shareable.

Sharing should never be limited to a single button at the bottom of the page.

---

# Share Formats

The MVP should support:

## 1. Social Image

A shareable image containing:

* Headline.
* Visual.
* One-sentence explanation.
* El País Está Fatal branding.
* Website URL.

Optimised for:

* WhatsApp.
* Telegram.
* X.
* Forums.

---

## 2. Copy Text

One click should copy a short, well-written response that users can immediately paste into conversations.

This feature should appear as:

> **Responder con esto**

rather than:

> Copy

The copied text should:

* Sound natural.
* Be concise.
* Avoid sounding institutional.
* Include enough context to stand on its own.
* Encourage readers to investigate further.

Example:

"La evidencia disponible muestra que la situación es más compleja de lo que suele afirmarse. Los datos indican X, pero también existen factores como Y y Z que cambian la interpretación. Puedes ver el análisis completo en elpaisestafatal.es."

---

## 3. Social Preview

When users share a page URL, it should generate an attractive social preview with:

* Title.
* Description.
* Featured image.
* Topic branding.

---

# Branding

Every shared visual should include:

* El País Está Fatal logo.
* Website URL.

Branding should be discreet.

It should not dominate the visual or reduce credibility.

Avoid excessive promotional elements.

---

# Visual Design

Different content types should have different layouts.

Examples:

Charts

↓

Large visual.

Minimal text.

Statistics

↓

Big number.

Small explanation.

Comparison tables

↓

Clear comparison.

Timeline

↓

Horizontal progression.

Misconceptions

↓

Question and explanation.

Each visual should still belong to the same visual family.

---

# Share Placement

Every important block should include sharing actions.

Examples:

Chart

* Share
* Responder con esto

Statistic

* Share
* Responder con esto

Timeline

* Share
* Responder con esto

Misconception

* Share
* Responder con esto

The page itself should also include a general Share button.

---

# "Responder con esto"

This becomes one of the defining features of the website.

Instead of simply sharing a link, users can immediately reuse a concise evidence-based explanation.

Purpose:

Help users participate in conversations without having to summarise the article themselves.

The generated response should:

* Be conversational.
* Avoid aggressive language.
* Avoid sounding like propaganda.
* Distinguish facts from interpretation.
* Encourage curiosity.

Responses should normally contain:

* One or two short paragraphs.
* Optional link to the full page.

---

# Universal Assets

The MVP will generate universal share assets.

There will not yet be separate versions for:

* WhatsApp.
* Telegram.
* X.
* Instagram.
* LinkedIn.

Future versions may optimise assets for each platform individually.

---

# Sources

Sources will not appear inside shared assets.

Instead:

* The website page contains the full references.
* Shared assets encourage readers to visit the complete analysis.

This keeps visuals cleaner while preserving transparency.

---

# User Journey

Claim Page

↓

Interesting chart

↓

Share

↓

Image generated

↓

Sent through messaging app or social media

↓

Recipient understands the key idea

↓

Optional visit to the website

---

# Success Criteria

The sharing system succeeds when:

* Users naturally share individual knowledge assets rather than only complete pages.
* Shared assets are understandable without opening the website.
* Shared arguments remain nuanced and evidence-based.
* The visuals encourage curiosity.
* Website traffic increases as a consequence of useful content, not as the primary objective.

---

# Future Improvements

Not part of the MVP:

* Platform-specific assets.
* Animated charts.
* Short vertical videos.
* Automatically generated social threads.
* Share analytics.
* AI-generated responses adapted to different audiences.
* Shareable podcast clips.
* Video explainers.
* Customisable share cards.
